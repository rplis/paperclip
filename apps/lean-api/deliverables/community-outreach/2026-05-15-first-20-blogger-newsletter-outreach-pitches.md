## Product Context Used

EPUBtoEPUB is positioned as a focused EPUB translation workflow: upload an `.epub`, choose a target language, generate a free preview, review formatting/tone, pay only if satisfied, and download a translated EPUB. This batch targets language-learning readers, ebook/newsletter operators, indie author resource sites, and translation communities where a free-preview EPUB translation workflow can be useful without overclaiming quality.

## Send Guardrails

- Send only from an approved human/brand email account.
- Do not imply sponsorship, partnership, or payment unless approved separately.
- Disclose affiliation clearly.
- Ask for consideration, a resource mention, or editorial feedback; do not demand coverage.
- Avoid claiming literary/publishing-grade translation. Position the tool for personal reading, study, drafts, public-domain books, and preview-first evaluation.
- Use one tracked URL per pitch.

## Manual Send Checklist

1. Confirm the target has a visible contact form, submission page, or accepted social/contact channel.
2. Replace `[tracked URL]` with the target-specific URL from the table.
3. Add sender identity and affiliation: "I work on EPUBtoEPUB."
4. Keep the message under 140 words unless the target asks for detailed submissions.
5. Log send date, sender account, response, and any published link.
6. Follow up once after 6-8 business days only if the target seems editorially relevant.
